Pub Crawl History

It began in 2003 as a small journey among close friends and has blossomed over the years to become the defining event of the summer.

The Neighborhood Pub Crawl features two zip codes of one of a kind destinations. We've got limited edition custom t-shirts, gaming, food, a house band, raffles, romance, marriages, babies, a dancy party, Captain Fun, the Chalice of Valor...you get the point.
