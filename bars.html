Mary's Place
approximate time: 1:00 PM - 3:45 PM

Mary's Place is the staging area and starting point on our journey and few would have it any other way. Hosting the annual bags tournament and boasting ice cold, dirt cheap Old Style bottles, snacks, scenic mural covered walls, a cozy "gaming" area, a spacious, yet picturesque beer garden, and the friendliest of staff, Mary's little slice of heaven is sure to leave you begging the timekeeper for a few extra minutes right out of the gate.

Activities: early bird bags tournament, outdoor patio, meet and greet, jumbo jenga, hot dogs, t-shirt pickup, golden ticket raffle, group photo

Filonek's
approximate time: 3:50 PM - 4:35 PM

Recently renovated with large, garage style front windows Filonek's might surprise even the most veteran of crawlers. With boxed wines, arcade machines, Okocim by the barrel, outdoor seating, and a former Bartender of the Month, this neighborhood sleeper goes straight to the head of the class for those crawlers of Polish descent. You'll want to belly on up to the bar, as it's a preferred parking area for all those down with the white eagle.

Activities: pool, arcade style gaming, outdoor patio

The Garage
approximate time: 4:40 PM - 5:25 PM

Owned and operated by Norwood Park residents, the Garage opened in 2011 with a friendly welcome from the neighborhood. Patrons can enjoy a game of shuffleboard, traditional darts, or their deep beer selection. Their Vegas style rooftop deck seats 50; has a 70 inch TV and has a designated smoking section for any puffers out there. Who's pounding?

Activities: shuffleboard, darts, smoking, root beer beer, 120 minute, rooftop action

Jet's Public House
approximate time: 5:30 PM - 6:15 PM

Formerly the site of Wally's, Jet's is a favorite with the neighborhood youngsters, and offers up some decent pub grub. You'll want to hold your appetite for our next stop as our food there is paid for, however, must you indulge, try the tots. Grab a board game from the back and enjoy some good times with your friends. If you get the chance, I'd recommend a game of Taboo with Cheesy.

Activities: competitive board gaming, jukebox, sports on tv, darts

The Three Counties Irish Pub
approximate time: 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM

A fan favorite and host like no other. Opening their loving arms to our annual H.O.Y.H.I.M.S.F.Y. jam, this authentic Irish gem pours a dynamite pint of the black stuff. Throw some darts, peruse the jukebox, enjoy the beer garden, and chat up the friendly staff about anything Irish. Top Notch. Formerly Snuggles Pub...oh man, if these walls could talk!

Activities: rock, H.O.Y.H.I.M.S.F.Y., outdoor patio, pizza

Gladstone Lounge & Liquors
approximate time: 8:10 PM - 8:55 PM

Gladstone Lounge & Liquors is what they had in mind when the term "diamond in the rough" was coined. Pool tables, locals, karaoke, NASCAR, and masterful woodwork combine to create a one-of-a-kind atmosphere rivaled by nothing. Honoring more drink specials than you can imagine, it's hard to leave disappointed from the endless comforts of the Lounge. Their bathroom is one of a kind.

Activities: pool, drink specials, admiring, pay phone

The Thatch Pub
approximate time: 9:00 PM - 11:45 PM

The Thatch gives Three Counties a run for its money when it comes to the black stuff. I'd suggest a pint to start things off. They upped their game in 2015 by hosting our legendary dance party and Chalice presentation. Things went off without a hitch and the change of location was met with rave reviews. Bring your A game and attmept to answer Captain Fun's prayers. He'll take you there.

Activities: dancing, Guinness, lasers, Chalice presentation

Vaughan’s Pub & Grill
approximate time: 12:00 AM - ?

Vaughan's Pub & Grill rounds out our crawl and kindly serves as our final destination. This is where it all began, folks. The namesake of our coveted Chalice, Vaughan's hosted our legendary dance party in their luxurious upstairs party suite for many many years. Make sure you ask around for the owner, Anne, and thank her at every turn for being awesome.

Activities: Guinness, jukebox, DJs, booths, craic, goodbyes
