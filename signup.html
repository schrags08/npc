What Does Registration Get Me?

Most importantly, the official Neighborhood Pub Crawl T-Shirt. These puppies are limited edition, custom designed, soft as hell, and built for comfort. Coveted stuff. As always, the artwork will remain secret until crawl day.

In addition to that, you get food during our stop at Three Counties. Papa Joe's Pizza, a neighborhood favorite, provides their legendary pie for our mouths.

But wait, there's more...you get a shot at winning the Golden Ticket and are automatically entered into our one of a kind Neighborhood Pub Crawl Raffle.

Registration fees also help cover the extensive Rider requests of the infamous HOYHIMSFY green room as well as keep Captain Fun funded and on the lookout to please.

All proceeds go back into the event. I make no profit.

Shirts are picked up at the first bar, Mary's Place. I will not be carrying the backpack of shirts around this year like a dope. If you want yours, get to the first bar on time. Don't blow it.
