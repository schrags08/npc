General Info:

2:00 PM -- The PUB CRAWL BEGINS at Mary's Place.

Mary's serves as the center for t-shirt distribution and marks the beginning of our fellowship.

Please show up if you want your t-shirt. I'm not bringing the backpack of unclaimed shirts out this year because it sucks and I like to enjoy the crawl too. If you ordered one, show up to get it. The shirts are a big part of NPC tradition, please respect that.

Mary's Place also hosts our world famous Golden Ticket raffle. The drawing begins after the arrival of our 30th patron. Winner claims $100 party cash. Come enjoy the friendly confines.

Our timekeeper runs a tight ship. Don't be late. It's best to not anger him early.

Here's the tentative time schedule:

1:00pm - 2:00pm - OPTIONAL - Mary's Place Welcome Picnic
2:00pm - 3:45pm - Mary's Place
3:50pm - 4:50pm - Filonek's
4:55pm - 5:40pm - The Garage
5:40pm - 6:35pm - Jet's
6:45pm - 10:05pm - Three Counties (pizza, band, chalice presentation, dance party)

I met with all the bars and they are excited for us to come, they know me and know that we always have a good group of people. Please be respectful of the bars and the surrounding neighborhoods. It's appreciated by all. Keep it classy.

1:00 PM (optional)

This honor is bestowed annually upon the day's finest crawler. It is a majestic, 11" chalice fit for any elegant occasion. The Chalice of Valor embodies qualities including spirit, style, sportsmanship, participation, and class. In order to be eligible for this coveted award one must make it all the way to the dance party. The winner is chosen by the Council of Chalice Elders, not by me. Seek their great wisdom to win. Bring thy heat.

If you happen to win this coveted treasure...it is yours for the year, to be returned at the next crawl to be passed on through the generations. Also, next year's registration fee is on the house.
